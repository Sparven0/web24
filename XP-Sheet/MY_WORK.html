How I did it

Photoshop

Photoshop is probably the most famous and widely used photo-editing software in the world, and with good reason. Me, like so many others have spent three years learning how to master Photoshop. And am I glad I did!

Photoshop is along with Ai one of my favorite softwares from Adobe. It's easy, reliable and endlessly customizable. The Adobe Creative-Cloud user-interface is more or less the same thru all Adobe softwares which sort of means I master all of them!

In-Design

Adobe In-Design is used for creating professional article-layouts and other layouts where text and images need to be good looking, easily readable and professional.

Personally, I haven't used ID as much as PS and Ai, but I am still comfortable working in it as Adobe's "user interface" is very developed which makes it both easy and powerful to use.

Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a powerful tool in creating logos, icons and other vector files. I have three years of experience in the program and here are some things I have done with it:

  • Making logos
  • Book-covers
  • Menues
  • Graphic Designs

Illustrator is definetaly one of my favorite programs in the Adobe Creative-Cloud library!
